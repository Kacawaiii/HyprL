# HyprL v0 Product Spec

## What HyprL is

- A generic signal service for US equities on 1h bars.
- Signals are informational only and not personalized advice.

## What the user gets

- Signal access (Discord and/or API).
- Public track record and dashboard.
- Basic onboarding and documentation.

## What the user does NOT get

- No execution on user accounts.
- No personalization or portfolio management.
- No guarantee of performance.

## Risk disclosure

Users must read and accept the risk disclaimer:
- `docs/legal/DISCLAIMER.md`

## Requirements to launch

- Track record in paper (minimum 2 weeks).
- Live micro track record (4 to 6 weeks).
- Public dashboard working from report artifacts.
